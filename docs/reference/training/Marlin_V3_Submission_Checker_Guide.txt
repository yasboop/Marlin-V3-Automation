Marlin-Submission-Checker-V3 — Optional but Recommended

Snorkel built a companion tool to help you catch common issues before you submit
each turn — rather than finding out after the fact during review. Think of it as a
live sanity check as you work through a task.


================================================================================
TOOL WALKTHROUGH
================================================================================

How to access
  Go to the Snorkel Expert Platform, find Marlin-Submission-Checker-V3, and click on
  a submission there.

How it works
  The tool is a scratchpad project — you do not submit anything through it.

  As you complete each turn, fill in the same fields you would enter in the CLI: your
  prompt, Model A and B pros/cons, and your SxS ratings. Then hit the feedback button to
  run automated checks on the spot.

What it currently flags
  • Your overall justification favoring one model while your SxS scores favor the
    other
  • Ratings that are not explained anywhere in your written feedback
  • Prompts that reference the PR directly
  • Follow-up prompts that repeat what was already asked in Turn 1

Multi-Turn Prompt Checker
  There is a second tab for multi-turn prompt checking: paste your prompts across turns
  and it will flag redundant requests or scope that drifts too far from the original
  task.

A few things to keep in mind
  • The tool is optional — nothing here auto-rejects your submission.
  • The checks are still experimental; you may occasionally disagree with a result. If
    so, flag it in the Slack channel.
  • Think of flagged issues as a preview of what reviewers will look for. A green
    light here is a good sign you are on the right track.
  • Once everything looks good, copy your values over into the CLI and submit as
    normal.
